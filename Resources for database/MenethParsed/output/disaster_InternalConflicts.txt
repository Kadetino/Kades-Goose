
=== 0 "Internal Conflicts ===
* Cannot fire randomly

'''Mean time to happen'''

* Base mean time to happen of 1 days

'''Immediate effects:'''

* Hidden effect:
** Sets the country flag had_internal_conflicts
* Changes stability by -2
* Random owned province:
** Limited to:
*** The province is not the capital of the country
*** fort_level = 1
** Spawns revolt of size 2 of 0 "Noble rebels
* Random owned province:
** Limited to:
*** The province is not the capital of the country
** Spawns revolt of size 2 of 0 "Peasant rebels

=== 0 "Religious Struggles ===

'''Trigger:'''

* Does not have the country modifier "0 "Religious Intolerance"
* Does not have the country modifier "0 "Religious Tolerance"
* Any owned province:
** The province is not overseas
** Does not follow the religion of its owner
* Is at war
* Cannot fire randomly

'''Mean time to happen'''

* Base mean time to happen of 1 days

'''Option: 0 "One nation, one belief!:'''

* Changes diplomatic power stored by -50
* Adds the modifier "0 "Religious Intolerance" for 10 years:
** +1% global heretic missionary strength
** -2 tolerance of heretics
** -2 tolerance of heathens

'''Option: 0 "Tolerate all beliefs:'''

* Changes administrative power stored by -50
* Adds the modifier "0 "Religious Tolerance" for 10 years:
** -10% stability cost modifier
** +1 tolerance of heretics
** +1 tolerance of heathens
** -2 tolerance of the true faith
