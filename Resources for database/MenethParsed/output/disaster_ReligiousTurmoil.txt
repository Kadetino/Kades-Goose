
=== 0 "Religious Civil Disorder ===
* Cannot fire randomly

'''Mean time to happen'''

* Base mean time to happen of 1 days

'''Immediate effects:'''

* Hidden effect:
** Sets the country flag active_religious_turmoil

'''Option: 0 "Eradicate the heretics to bring peace:'''

* Random owned province:
** Limited to:
*** Is in the 0 "Christian religion group
*** Does not follow the religion of its owner
** Spawns revolt of size 3 of 0 "Religious rebels
* If:
** Limited to:
*** has_disaster = 0 "Religious Turmoil
** Adds the modifier "0 "Intolerant Society" for the rest of the campaign:
*** +2% global heretic missionary strength
*** -2 tolerance of heretics

'''Option: 0 "We should strive for a tolerant society:'''

* If:
** Limited to:
*** has_disaster = 0 "Religious Turmoil
** Adds the modifier "0 "Tolerant Society" for the rest of the campaign:
*** +50% stability cost modifier
*** +1 tolerance of heretics
