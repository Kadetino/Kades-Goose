=== 0 "Governmental Incompetence ===
* Cannot fire randomly

** Base chance of 50
* Every owned province:
** Limited to:
*** Most likely rebel type is 0 "Revolutionary

*** Most likely rebel type is 0 "Revolutionary
*** The province is not overseas

=== 0 "Battles on Foreign Ground ===

'''Trigger:'''

* Is at war
* Has at least 1 allies
* Any of the following:
** Has at least 5 active loans
** Inflation is less than 5
** 5 war exhaustion
* Does not have the country modifier "0 "Decreased Morale"
* Does not have the country modifier "0 "Reduced War Expenditures"
* Cannot fire randomly

'''Mean time to happen'''

* Base mean time to happen of 1 days

'''Option: 0 "The treasury isn't even close to empty:'''

* Random ally:
** Adds the opinion modifier "0 "Munificent Ally" towards our country
* If:
** Limited to:
*** has_disaster = 0 "Aspiration for Liberty
** Adds the modifier "0 "Decreased Morale" for the rest of the campaign:
*** -10% land morale
*** -10% naval morale

'''Option: 0 "Cut down on war expenditures:'''

* If:
** Limited to:
*** has_disaster = 0 "Aspiration for Liberty
** Adds the modifier "0 "Reduced War Expenditures" for the rest of the campaign:
*** +0.1 war exhaustion
* Random ally:
** Adds the opinion modifier "0 "Reduced War Expenditures" towards our country
